What is cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity refers to the process of keeping technological systems safe from outside forces. Successful cybersecurity efforts can protect sensitive information and allow organizations and businesses to build trust with their customers. Any organization that uses technology and regularly collects information uses some form of cybersecurity to keep that information safe.

Job Overview

We are looking for an experienced Automotive Cybersecurity Engineer with a strong background in embedded systems, cybersecurity standards, and hands-on experience in cybersecurity development for ECUs. The candidate should have a certification in ISO/SAE 21434, experience in analyzing cybersecurity specifications, designing security systems, and working with various cybersecurity testing methods. Proficiency in C, C++, and good English communication skills are required.
